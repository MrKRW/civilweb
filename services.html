Comprehensive Solutions

Design & Visualization

  • Architectural Design: Professional design and drawings documentation for residential, commercial, Industrial and Mixed use projects.

Engineering & Infrastructure
02

Engineering & Infrastructure

  • Structural Engineering Support: Structural designs, drafting, detailing, structural calculation reports, soil investigation coordination, and council submission drawings.
  • Civil Engineering & External Works: Design documentation for site development, drainage, and infrastructure.
  • MEP & Building Services Coordination: Drafting and coordination for mechanical, electrical, plumbing, and smart systems, HVAC coordination, CCTV, solar panels, and multi-disciplinary drawings.
Management, Costing & Consulting

Management, Costing & Consulting

Project Close-Out

End-to-End Turnkey Construction
